Workday2Calendar

Instructions:
  1. Go to your Current Classes page on Workday
  2. Click "Export to Excel" and download the exported file
  3. Upload the downloaded file here
  4. After upload, you can choose to either
    • sign in & export directly to google calendar
    • convert to an ICS file that you can import to your local calendar
